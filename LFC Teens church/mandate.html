The divine vision that birthed Living Faith Church Worldwide.

The Mandate

“The hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith, and I am sending you to undertake this task.”

This divine mandate was delivered to God’s servant, Bishop David Oyedepo, on May 2nd, 1981, marking the beginning of the Living Faith Church Worldwide (also known as Winners’ Chapel).

Since then, the church has remained committed to spreading the message of faith, liberation, and victorious living in Christ across the globe.
