About Us

Worthy Ambassadors of Christ, shining His light to our world.

We are a people of grace, glory, and power set to shake the world for Jesus, and represent Him as Worthy Ambassadors of the Kingdom.

As Kingdom Ambassadors, we represent Christ in deed and character, made worthy of our calling as we grow and aspire for excellence, in atmosphere of worship, praise, and soul-lifting encounters that pervade our services.

Matthew 6:33 drives our conviction as we seek first the things of God's kingdom, so that all other wonderful things are added to us in our glorious journey.

We are happy to receive you and welcome you to our midst to enjoy the warmth and fellowship under Christ's love.
